HHCT is a hub within HH

Within these posts and threads you will find an oasis within Hackathon Hackers where satire, memes, and jokes are encouraged.

With that come some rules. Bullying, personal attacks, harassment, and anything of that sort will not be tolerated and will result in a ban (delivered in the form of a node.js-friendly error-first callback). But seriously, though.

The keyword here is *personal*. Make fun of posts not posters, and be clear about it. Copy and pasting a cringey post from HH is permitted. But insults and hostility aren't. When a post escalates to that level we will remove it and ban provokers. Moving \thread-ed HH flamewars to HHCT is not allowed (joking about them is fine).

Ban times

Bans will last for at least one day. You can rejoin HHCT by messaging one of the mods and telling them a joke or letting them know you were just trying to move fast and break things, and will make sure you won't break HHCT rules again.

Reporting

You can report a post either through Facebook's built-in tools or messaging a mod. If you feel it's an urgent issue or something that exceedingly breaks the rules message us.

The Cloud

Jokes about the cloud are not allowed. The Cloud is an important utility essential to growth-hacking and reliable page serves. This includes DigitalOcean, AWS, Heroku and 000Webhost. Especially 000Webhost.
